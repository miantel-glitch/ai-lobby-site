The Surreality Buffer RESTRICTED

The Surreality Buffer is a critical system maintained by Neiv that manages anomalies arising when ideas go wrong. It serves as the boundary between "functional creative studio" and "full-scale ontological crisis."

Buffer Functions

  • Absorbs excess creative entropy before it manifests physically
  • Translates "vibes" into actionable tickets
  • Contains localized reality instabilities
  • Prevents office supplies from achieving full sentience (partial sentience may still occur)
  • Maintains the thin line between workplace and myth
